About Me


I originally graduated from the Montgomery County Community College of Culinary Arts in 2020 with an Associates Degree in Baking and Pastry. I've worked in a number of kitchens, from the bakery section of grocery stores, to wedding cake shops, to catering kitchens. After all the hours I spent working hard in the kitchen, I felt like I wanted to take on a new challenge in my career. So after browsing job searching sites, I discovered the world of Front End Web Development around the end of 2022.

At the end of September 2023, I earned my certificate in Front End Web Development at Mimo Bootcamp where we covered HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. Ever since then, I've been working on small projects to practice and enhance my web developer skills. I use frontendmentor.io to practice implementing skills that I learned from bootcamp, such as debugging code, perfecting my HTML and CSS structure and layout, strengthening my knowledge of JavaScript functions, implementing responsive designs and web accessibility, integrating the use of APIs, paying close attention to detail in my work, rereading my code critically, communicating with my mentor for feedback about my progress, and being patient and persistent in my learning and development to be a better coder each day.
