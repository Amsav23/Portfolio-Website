I'm your digital chef, Amyln!


Originally, I graduated from Montgomery County Community College of Culinary Arts in 2020 with an Associate Degree in Baking and Pastry. My experience spans various kitchens, from the bakery section in grocery stores, to wedding cake shops and catering kitchens. After dedicating countless hours working diligently in the kitchen, I realized I was ready for a new challenge in my career. Towards the end of 2022, while exploring job opportunities, I discovered the fascinating world of Front End Web Development.

By the end of September 2023, I earned my certificate in Front End Web Development from Mimo Bootcamp, where I developed a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. Since then, I've been working on various real-world solution based projects to continuously practice and refine my front-end development skills. I frequently utilize frontendmentor.io to apply these skills, tackling challenges like debugging code, implementing responsive designs and web accessibility, critically analyzing my code, and creating dynamic, interactive web experiences using JavaScript libraries like React. Each project strengthens my abilities, helping me grow and adapt as a developer every day.
